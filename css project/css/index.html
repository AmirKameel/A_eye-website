Our Popular solution

Performance Analysis

The platform collects data and statistics on player performance in live matches. This is done by using artificial intelligence techniques to analyze player movements and estimate their positions on the field. This data is analyzed to identify each player's strengths and weaknesses, and how they can improve their performance.

Talent Discovery

The platform uses artificial intelligence to discover young talents in football. Machine learning techniques are used to analyze player performance in matches and training, and to search for performance patterns similar to those of professional players.Finding the next football superstar is a crucial aspect of the business side of the sport.
